What Is Detty December ?

"Dirty December" is a slang term referring to a period of time, typically the month of December, where individuals engage in more casual all types of activities. It's often associated with a loosening of social norms and increased opportunities for socializing during the holiday season. The term implies a less restrictive approach to relationships and intimacy compared to other times of the year.
